How To Use

We designed this site simple so that everyone can easily use our services.

Step 1: Click on start now.
Step 2: Choose a subject (for example: buy a credit card or transfer PayPal or ...)
Step 3: Enter the total amount you want to pay into Bitcoin.
Step 4: Enter the buyer email. (Your email)
Step 5: Enter the seller's email.
Step 6: Enter the website you want to buy from (If the seller does not have a website, You can, not fill in this field.)
Step 7: Choose how long the package will be delivered to you. (Or any services they provide to you for how long they should provide you)
Step 8: Click Continue.
Step 9: You will be shown a bitcoin address where you have the opportunity to pay the desired amount for one hour.

After entering the information and making the payment, the information you entered will be registered in our system.

We will send a code to your email and the seller's email after a maximum of 15 minutes. This is a code for the contract between you and the seller.

We will get the Bitcoin address from the seller and transfer 10% of the amount you paid us to the seller's Bitcoin account.

And every time the seller delivers the package to you or provides you with the services it should have provided, we will transfer 90% of the rest of the amount you paid us to the seller's Bitcoin account.

If for any reason the delivery is not delivered to the buyer or the seller intends to scam, we will transfer 90% to the buyer's Bitcoin account and return the money to the buyer.

If the package does not reach the buyer, all you have to do is email us and we will return your money to your Bitcoin account.

Once the transaction is complete, the buyer must pay us $ 5 for the services we provide.
Make any transaction and with any amount you want, we take $5 for each transaction from buyer.
