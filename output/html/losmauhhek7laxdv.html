The Darknet is a growing community of people who look for security, privacy and to be free of government control. This market was developed because we realized shopping on darknet was very complicated. Escrow Policy

Every buyer on this market is protected with 30 day escrow policy. When you pay for your order, your funds will be securely freezed on hold for 30 days. If you don't receive ordered goods in 30 days, all your funds will be refunded with no hesitation.

Vendors who sell goods that require shipping, use DHL or FedEx WW shipping that usualy takes 3-7 days. You will also be given a tracking number for every order you make. If less than 30 days have gone since your order and you have not received the goods please contact us immediately and provide us with your order ID.
