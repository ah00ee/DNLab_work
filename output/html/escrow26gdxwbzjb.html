How to use

5 simple steps to safely use the escrow.

1) Create Escrow

Use the destination Bitcoin address, amount to hold and two passwords to create your own Escrow. Will fully expire after 10 days from creation.

2) Save the Data

Save the Escrow ID, addresses and both passwords. Share the view password and id, while keeping the unlock one private and secured.

3) Load Escrow

Send the btc amount shown on screen, 2% fee is included. 0.03 and 200 btc are the min and max limits. Only 1 network confirmation is required.

4) Check Status

The other party can check the Escrow status using his view-only password, to see the amount deposited and his address as destination of funds.

5a) Unlock Funds

After you received what you purchased from the other party you can unlock the funds using the password. Irreversible.

5b) Refund Escrow

After 8 days the Escrow will automatically refund you on the same address used to fund the Escrow (source). Irreversible.

Example 1

Buyer opens an Escrow with Seller address as destination. Seller ships item, buyer receives it and unlocks funds.

Example 2

If Seller doesnt ship the item Buyer will get automatically refunded on his source address at Escrow expiration.
