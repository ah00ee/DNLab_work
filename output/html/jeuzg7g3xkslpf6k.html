Your payments are protected by law.

Money you pay into our client accounts is segregated and ring-fenced by the Payment Services Regulations 2009 and can only be used for your benefit. In the event anything happens to Biscrow, this law totally safeguards your money. Please see Regulation 19 of the Payment Services Regulations 2009.
