How it works:

An escrow service acts like a guarantor in transactions between buyer and seller. The buyer deposit the funds in the third party escrow service account and they remain locked. The buyer always have total control of his bitcoins. He can check the status in every moment. When the buyer get the products then he can release the bitcoins and pay the seller.

Guarantees for the buyer: ensuring you get exactly what you ordered or your money back.
Funds are only released once you have received your goods as agreed

Guarantees for the seller: guaranteeing that you receive all the money for your sale
Eliminate financial fraud and scams while securing the buyer's payment at closing

Guarantees for both: guaranteeing total privacy on your trading
